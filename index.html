The DBAC Platform offers an easy and fast way to analyse barcoded sequencing data to identify CITES species.

The rapidly increasing need to identify species and to get their taxonomic information motivates scientists to develop new approaches in order to make the analysis of mixed DNA samples easier. A Platform that sums up the whole pipeline to analyse multiplexed data, can avoide users any conflicts with UNIX by offering an easy and effective way to follow the analysis process until getting the final product ready for blast.

DBAC Platform is dedicated for PacBio sequencing data and it is mainly based on the SMRT Link tools that work independently on SMRT Link software installation.

You need to provide information about the sequencing data to proceed the analysis.

After you have successfully uploaded your input files, press the button 'Submit' to run the analysis.

Be patient untill you are prompted with how to proceed for downloading your output data.

Also note that the computations may take a while depending on your data size.
